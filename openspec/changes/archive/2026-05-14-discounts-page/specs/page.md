## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: NavLink and route

The app SHALL have a /descuentos route using ClientShell, and the navbar SHALL use NavLink to="/descuentos" with isActive styling.

#### Scenario: Navigation to Descuentos

- WHEN the user clicks "Descuentos" in the navbar
- THEN the user navigates to /descuentos
- AND the navbar link shows active state (text-primary)
